The Scenario

"A car travels 60 km/h northward"

1

Step 1: What is Speed?

Speed is a scalar quantity. It has magnitude (size/value) only, with no direction.

2

Step 2: What is Velocity?

Velocity is a vector quantity. It is defined as speed in a specified direction. (Magnitude + Direction)

3

Step 3: Analyze the Scenario

Does this scenario state a direction? Yes, "northward".

4

Step 4: Conclusion

Therefore, the quantity described is Velocity.
