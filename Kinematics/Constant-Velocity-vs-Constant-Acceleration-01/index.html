Interactive Real-time Graphs

Note how the slopes (gradients) of these lines correspond to velocity and acceleration respectively.

Constant Velocity

It covers the exact same distance in every interval. Look at the ghost trails: they are perfectly spaced! Formula: s = vt. The s-t graph is a straight line with a constant slope.

Constant Acceleration

It starts slower (or static) but accelerates exponentially! Look at the ghost trails: they get further and further apart! Formula: s = ut + ½at². The s-t graph is a quadratic curve.
