Key Concept Insights

What defines Free Fall?

Free fall is the motion of an object falling solely under the influence of gravity. Here, acceleration is constant (g). Velocity increases by exactly g m/s every single second.

Observe the Strobe Trail

Notice how the visual ghost markers get further and further apart near the bottom. This expanding gap directly proves that the ball is accelerating, not moving at a constant speed.
