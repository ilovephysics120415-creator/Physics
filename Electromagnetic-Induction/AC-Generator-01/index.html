Key Exam concepts (6091 Syllabus)

As the coil rotates, it cuts magnetic flux. Continuous connection to the external circuit is maintained using slip rings and carbon brushes. Because each side of the coil moves up and down alternately, the direction of the induced current reverses every half-cycle, producing an alternating current (a.c.) voltage-time graph.
