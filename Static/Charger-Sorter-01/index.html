Two Types of Charges

Objects become charged when they gain or lose electrons. Protons are bound in the nucleus and do not move during charging!

1 C

S.I. Unit

Electric charge is measured in coulombs (C). One electron has a charge of approx. -1.6 × 10^{-19} C.

Law of Electrostatics

Like charges repel, unlike charges attract.
