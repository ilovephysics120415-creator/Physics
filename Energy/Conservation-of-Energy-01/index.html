Conservation of Energy

Singapore O-Level Physics Syllabus

Key Principle

Principle of Conservation of Energy

Energy cannot be created or destroyed. It can only be transferred from one store to another. The total energy in all stores always remains constant.

Ideal Situation

Frictionless

Energy transfers only between the gravitational potential store and the kinetic store.
