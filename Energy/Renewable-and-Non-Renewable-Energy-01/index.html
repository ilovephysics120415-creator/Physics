Evaluation Criteria

⚡

Efficiency of energy transfer

The ratio of useful electrical energy output to the total energy input from the resource store.

💰

Cost

Includes initial setup/construction, fuel supply, operational maintenance, and eventual decommissioning costs.

🔒

Reliability

The consistency of electricity supply. Stable resources run 24/7 regardless of fluctuating weather or daylight.

🌱

Environmental impact

The severity of greenhouse gas emissions, air/water pollution, land occupation, and ecosystem disruption.
