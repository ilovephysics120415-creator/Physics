Concept 1
Work Done
W = Fd
  • W = work done (in joules, J)
  • F = constant force applied (in newtons, N)
  • d = distance moved in the direction of the force (in metres, m)
Concept 2
Power
P = E / t
  • P = power (in watts, W)
  • E = energy transferred (in joules, J)
  • t = time taken (in seconds, s)
Concept 3
Efficiency
Eff = useful output⁄total input × 100%
  • efficiency = ratio of useful output to total input
  • expressed as a percentage (%)
  • applies to both energy (J) and power (W)
