O-LEVEL PHYSICS SIMULATOR
"A small force in. A large force out."

▶ Pressure is transmitted equally throughout a fluid (Pascal's Principle)

▶ A larger piston area means a larger output force

GOVERNING EQUATIONS
Pascal's Principle: P = F₁ ÷ A₁ = F₂ ÷ A₂
Output Force: F₂ = F₁ × (A₂ ÷ A₁)
REAL-WORLD APPLICATIONS
Car Brake System

Foot pedal pushes a small piston, generating high pressure to actuate large brake pads.

Dentist's Chair

Small manual or electric pump strokes create massive hydraulic lift to raise patients effortlessly.

Hydraulic Car Lift

Compressor pushes fluid through small inlet to raise tons of steel on a broad output piston.
