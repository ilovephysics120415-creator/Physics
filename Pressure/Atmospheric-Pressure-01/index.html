Why mercury?

Mercury is extremely dense (13,600 kg/m³): This keeps the vertical column height at a manageable ~760 mm tall.

Water comparison: Because water has a low density of 1,000 kg/m³, standard atmospheric pressure would support a column over 10 meters tall!
