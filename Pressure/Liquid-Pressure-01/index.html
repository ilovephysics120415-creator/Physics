Core Formulas

DENSITY FORMULA
ρ = m ÷ V

ρ = density (kg/m³)

m = mass (kg)

V = volume (m³)

LIQUID PRESSURE FORMULA
P = h ρ g

P = pressure (Pa)

h = depth (m)

ρ = density (kg/m³)

g = gravitational field strength (10 N/kg)

Key Insights

Liquid pressure increases with depth.

Liquid pressure does NOT depend on container shape.
