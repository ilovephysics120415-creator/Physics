Wave Speed Equation

The Wave Equation

v = f × λ
Wave Speed (v)
Unit: metres per second (m/s)

How fast the wave pattern propagates through space. Speed measures distance covered per second.

Frequency (f)
Unit: hertz (Hz)

How many complete waves pass a single reference point every second. 1 Hz = 1 wave/second.

Wavelength (λ)
Unit: metres (m)

The spatial length of one complete wave cycle (e.g. from crest to crest or trough to trough).
