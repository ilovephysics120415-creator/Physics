What is an Echo?

An echo is a reflected sound wave. Since the sound must travel to the reflecting surface and then travel all the way back, the total time measured is for the round trip. Divide the time by 2 to calculate the one-way distance.

Distance (d) =
Speed (v) × Time (t) 2
Where t is the total round-trip time, and v is the speed of sound.
Speed of Sound in Air 340 m/s
Speed of Sound in Water ~1500 m/s
