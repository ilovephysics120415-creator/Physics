Topic Overview

How Sound Waves Work

Sound travels as longitudinal waves. We represent them visually using transverse waveform diagrams to analyze their amplitude and frequency.

Loudness & Amplitude

Both waves have the same frequency, but different heights (amplitudes).

LOUD (Large Amplitude)
SOFT (Small Amplitude)
💡

Amplitude relates to Loudness. Larger amplitude = Louder sound.

Pitch & Frequency

Both waves have the same height, but different cycle spacings (frequencies).

HIGH PITCH (High Frequency)
LOW PITCH (Low Frequency)
💡

Frequency relates to Pitch. Higher frequency = Higher pitch.
