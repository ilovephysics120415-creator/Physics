Learn the functions, color codes, and safety configuration of standard UK 3-pin plugs.

🔌

Wire Functions & Hazards

  • Live (Brown): Delivers high alternating voltage (~230V) from the power source to the appliance. The most dangerous wire.
  • Neutral (Blue): Completes the circuit by returning current to the power source at near-zero voltage.
  • Earth (Green/Yellow): A low-resistance safety wire connecting the metal casing of the appliance to the ground, preventing electric shocks.
🏷️

Standard UK 3-Pin Plug

Pins are arranged as: Earth at the top, Neutral on the left, and Live on the right.

🛡️

Why Switch & Fuse are on the Live Wire

Placing safety devices on the Live wire disconnects the high voltage from the appliance, isolating it and preventing electrocution hazards if a fault occurs.
