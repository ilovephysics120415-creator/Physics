💡 Singapore syllabus Cheat Sheet (Lower Sec Science)

Live Wire (Brown): Connects appliance to high potential (240V in Singapore). Always houses the Fuse and Switch for isolating safety.
Neutral Wire (Blue): Completes the electric circuit. Maintained at a safe potential of approximately 0V.
Earth Wire (Green/Yellow): Connects metal casing directly to ground (0V). Acts as a low resistance escape path for fault currents.
