The Right-Hand Grip Rule

For a straight current-carrying wire, point your right thumb in the direction of the conventional current. Your curled fingers will point in the direction of the magnetic field lines.

O-Level Syllabus Tip (6091):
  • Field lines are concentric circles centered on the wire.
  • Spacing increases as you move further away, representing a weaker field.
  • Reversing current direction reverses the field direction.
  • Increasing current increases field strength (denser lines).
